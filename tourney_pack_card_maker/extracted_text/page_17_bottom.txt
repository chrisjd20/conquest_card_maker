Setting up the Battlefield
Place two 9" Objective Zone and one 6" Objective 
Zone as shown in the diagram.
Victory Points
In this Scenario Objective Zones can be Seized and 
points scored from Round 1. At the end of Rounds 
1-4 or 9-10 Players gain VPs as follows:
● Players gain 2 VPs for securing the 6" Objective Zone.
● Players gain 1 VP for securing the 9" Objective Zone.
At the end of Rounds 5-8 Players gain VPs as follows:
● Players gain 1 VPs for securing the 6” Objective Zone.
● Players gain 2 VP for securing the 9” Objective Zone.
Game Length
The game ends at the end of the 10th Round. The Player 
that has scored the most VP’s is declared the winner.
